Projects

Mental Health Stimulator

Technology that listens when someone needs it the most.

Many people hesitate to share their emotional struggles or seek help when facing stress or anxiety. This project explores how interactive software can provide a supportive space for reflection and guidance. Built using C++ with structured logical flows and data structures, the system simulates conversation-like interactions that analyze user inputs and generate thoughtful responses. By guiding users through meaningful prompts and personalized feedback, the application highlights how technology can encourage emotional awareness and promote better mental well-being.

Crop Disease Detector

Helping farmers detect crop diseases before they destroy the harvest.

Crop diseases often spread quickly and remain unnoticed until they significantly damage the harvest. This project investigates how machine learning can assist farmers in identifying plant diseases at an early stage using leaf images. Developed in Python using CNN, SVM, and KNN models along with TensorFlow, NumPy, Pandas, and OpenCV, the system processes leaf images and classifies potential diseases with improved accuracy. By enabling faster detection and offering actionable insights, the solution demonstrates how AI can support farmers in protecting crop health and improving agricultural productivity.
